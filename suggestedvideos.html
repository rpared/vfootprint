Going plant-based is a great way to reduce your carbon footprint and help the environment, and it's surprisingly easy to get started. Furthermore, embracing veganism introduces ethical considerations that provide a powerful and compelling motivation, which can make the transition even more straightforward. To help you on your journey, here are some tips and answers to frequently asked questions.
