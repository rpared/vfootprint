How much does a vegan save per day?

Numbers vary significantly depending on the country, the size and diet of the individual. The following estimates are an average of the world's consumption avoiding aproximations or inflated numbers. Using data from several sources, Our World in Data (animal consumption), Worldometer (human population), and Cowspiracy (environmental estimates), the following daily savings are observed for an average vegan:
  • 0.03 Land Animals1
  • 0.15 Lbs. of Sea Animals2
  • 20 Lbs. of C023
  • 1100 Lts. of water3
  • 45 Lbs. of grains3
By choosing a plant-based diet, individuals contribute to environmental sustainability and animal welfare daily.
